Hi, I'm Danielle!

I’m a Front End Web Developer with a passion for HTML and CSS. My interest in coding was sparked when, at the ripe old age of 12, I found myself tweaking background layouts for my MySpace page. As the years flew by and my love for programming grew, I searched out how I could turn my passion for coding into a career. And that’s when I stumbled upon The Iron Yard.

After receiving a full-ride scholarship through the #YesWeCode Fund, I graduated from The Iron Yard where I completed a full-stack immersive bootcamp, totaling in nearly 1,000 hours in lecture and 60-80 hour work weeks consisting of daily programming assignments and participating in practical projects. Of all the things I learned during my time at TIY, the most valuable was learning HOW to learn. Persistence pays off in the way of completed projects and pride in what I’ve created. How many people get to do what they are truly passionate about? Now I know at least one.
